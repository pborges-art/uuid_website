    What is UUID v4?
    🔐
    Cryptographically random
    Generated from 122 bits of secure random data. Virtually impossible to predict or guess.
    🌍
    Globally unique
    The probability of two UUIDs colliding is roughly 1 in 5.3×10³⁶ — for practical purposes, impossible.
    📐
    Standard format
    32 hex digits in 8-4-4-4-12 groups, defined by RFC 4122. Universally supported.
    ⚡
    No server needed
    Generated entirely in your browser using the Web Crypto API. Nothing leaves your device.
