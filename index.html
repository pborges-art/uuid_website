    What is UUID v4?
    🔐
    Cryptographically random
    Generated from 122 bits of secure random data — virtually impossible to predict.
    🌍
    Globally unique
    Collision probability is roughly 1 in 5.3×10³⁶. For practical purposes, impossible.
    📐
    Standard format
    32 hex digits in 8-4-4-4-12 groups. Defined by RFC 4122, universally supported.
    ⚡
    No server needed
    Generated by the Web Crypto API, entirely in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.
